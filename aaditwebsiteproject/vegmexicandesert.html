Veg Mexican Desserts

Churros are fried dough pastries coated with cinnamon and sugar, often served with a chocolate dipping sauce.
Flan is a creamy caramel custard dessert made from eggs, milk, and sugar.
Arroz con Leche is a comforting Mexican rice pudding made with rice, milk, sugar, and cinnamon.
Tres Leches Cake is a soft sponge cake soaked in a mixture of three types of milk: evaporated, condensed, and heavy cream.
Capirotada is a traditional Mexican bread pudding made with cinnamon, raisins, and syrup.
